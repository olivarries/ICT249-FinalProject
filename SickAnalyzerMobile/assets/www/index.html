About SAM

Information

Sickness Analyzer Mobile (SAM) is a mobile application that enables user determine the exact or the probable kind of disease or sickness experienced by a patient.

Instructions

  • To search for the symptoms and other information about a sickness, on the Search Page, just type the exact name of the disease and hit the Search button.
  • To determine the kind of disease experienced by a patient, just go to Analyzer Page, select the observation and symptoms seen in the patient, and hit the Analyze Button.
